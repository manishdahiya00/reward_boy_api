QuizBox App Privacy Policy

Please read the following privacy policy statements carefully before using the QuizBox App or Website. By using our services, you consent to the collection, use, and storage of data as described.

Personal Data We Collect

To use the QuizBox App/Website or to register/login, you need to provide the following information:

  • Mobile Number: Required for OTP verification, handling queries, and sending custom offers. Without it, we cannot provide our services.
  • Email Address: Used for service notifications, fraud detection, job alerts, and responding to queries. Consent is given by registering with us.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to enhance your experience and ensure the security of our services:

  • Mobile Device Information: Includes hardware details, unique identifiers, and network information to detect fraud and monitor app activities.
  • Log Data: Includes SMS messages for OTP, usage patterns, IP address, and system activities to improve the app and address issues.
  • Location Data: Used to provide location-based services and offers, improving your app experience.
  • Unique Application Numbers: Tracks app installs/uninstalls and monitors cashback offers.
  • Native Storage: Stores app data and caches to maintain login sessions and sync with our servers.
  • Cookies: Used for device classification and analytics to enhance our services.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your data outside QuizBox except in the following cases:

  • With Your Consent: Data may be shared with partners only if you approve, for personalization and analytics purposes.
  • External Processing: Data may be processed for analytical purposes or legal reasons.

Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies separately.

Children’s Privacy

We do not collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such information, we will delete it from our servers. Parents or guardians should contact us if they believe their child has provided personal information.

Referrals and Invites

If you use our referral services, we may collect your friends' contact details to send them an invitation. This information is used solely for the referral process.

Survey Technology

We use the Pollfish SDK for surveys. Pollfish collects device data and survey responses. For details, read Pollfish’s terms at Pollfish Respondent Terms. You can opt out via Pollfish’s website.
